# VCROSS()
`vcross(<vector1>, <vector2>[, <delimiter>])`

  Returns the 3-dimensional vector that is the cross product of its 3-dimensional argument vectors. The cross product is defined as:

   x = Ay * Bz - By * Az
   y = Az * Bx - Bz * Ax
   z = Ax * By - Bx * Ay

  > think vcross(4 5 6, 7 8 9)
  -3 6 -3
